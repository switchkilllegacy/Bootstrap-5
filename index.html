Inside the Experience...

A quick glance at what you'll learn and taste

Learn how a single energy drink evolved into a global lifestyle brand. We cover the early days, the iconic claw logo, and how Monster took over extreme sports and esports.

What exactly is giving you that rush? We break down the science behind the blend—discussing caffeine, taurine, B-vitamins, and how zero-sugar alternatives maintain their kick.

This is where the fun begins. We teach you how to properly taste and identify the subtle notes in different series, from the citrus punch of the Originals to the smooth finishes of the Rehab line.

Did you know certain flavors pair perfectly with specific snacks? We'll show you how to mix non-alcoholic mocktails using your favorite Monster Energy bases.

At the end of every tasting, attendees get exclusive access to our private pop-up shop. Grab limited edition gear, apparel, and merchandise that you can't find online.
